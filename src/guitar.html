Structural

Root (R): The absolute home base. Complete resolution.
Perfect 5th: Power and stability. The strongest support for the root.

Chord Quality

Major 3rd / Maj7: Bright, open character. Defines major harmony.
Minor 3rd / Min7: Deeper, introspective. Defines minor harmony.

Extensions

2nd / 6th: Airy, floating color tones. Dreamy and unresolved.
4th (sus): Structural ambiguity. Yearns to resolve to the 3rd.

Altered

b2 / b6: Acidic friction. Half-step above chord tones. Exotic suspense.
Tritone (b5): Maximum symmetrical tension. Piercing, demands resolution.
